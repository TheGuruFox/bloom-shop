Our Story

Born in a kitchen.
Grown with care.

Bloom started when our founder, frustrated by endless ingredient labels she couldn't read, decided to make her own soap. That first batch — a simple lavender and olive oil bar — was given to neighbours, then neighbours' neighbours.

Five years on, we still make every product by hand in our Cape Town studio, still read every ingredient label, and still believe that what goes on your skin should be as honest as what goes in your body.
